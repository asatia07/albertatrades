AlbertaTrades.com is a website to help people compare between the different trades available in Alberta and search for jobs.

Ideally, it is a place where somebody looking to move to Alberta for work can go to get a better idea of what trade they should enter.

It is an ongoing project, so any feedback you can provide for features, improvements, or suggestions are appreciated.
